Grandma's Pomodoro

prep 10 min cook 35 min serves 6 tags italian · vegetarian · weeknight

A slow-simmered tomato sauce built on soffritto, San Marzano tomatoes, and the last crush of summer basil. Keeps a week in the fridge; freezes beautifully.

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 2 × 28oz San Marzano tomatoes
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • ½ tsp sugar
  • 1 handful fresh basil, torn

Instructions

  1. Warm oil over medium; sweat onion 8 min until soft and translucent.
  2. Add garlic; cook 1 min until fragrant — do not brown.
  3. Crush tomatoes by hand into the pot. Add salt and sugar.
  4. Simmer uncovered 25–30 min, stirring occasionally, until thickened.
  5. Off heat, stir in basil. Taste, adjust salt, and serve.
