Thai Basil Beef

Prep5 min
Cook15 min
Serves4
Tagsthai · weeknight · basil

Minced beef stir-fried hot and fast with garlic, chilis, and a big handful of holy basil. Over jasmine rice with a runny fried egg — about twenty minutes, start to table.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20)
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2–3 Thai chilies, sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 cup holy or Thai basil leaves
  • 4 eggs, for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat a wok or large skillet over high until smoking.
  2. Add oil, then garlic and chilies — stir-fry 15 seconds.
  3. Add beef; break up and cook until browned and crisp in spots.
  4. Stir in soy, oyster sauce, and sugar. Toss to coat.
  5. Off heat, fold in basil until just wilted.
  6. Serve over jasmine rice with a fried egg on top.
