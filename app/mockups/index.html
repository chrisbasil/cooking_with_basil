Cooking with Basil — three directions

Three lightweight mockups of the recipe library, each leaning a different way on warmth, botanical motif, and kitchen iconography. Same dummy recipes in all three, so you can compare apples-to-apples.

Hybrid — Brighter Bistro ✦

Trattoria tactile cards + Modern Bistro header, search, and kitchen icons. Brighter palette: coral, slate-blue, basil green on cream. The current direction.

Trattoria Warmth

Italian-kitchen coziness. Sienna + cream, chalkboard filter chips, hand-drawn basil sprigs on every card, Playfair + Caveat script. Boldest and most appetite-forward of the three.

Garden Kitchen

Botanical and airy. Sage-forward, watercolor basil leaves drifting in on load, Cormorant italic display. Fresh daylight feeling — farmer's market rather than restaurant.

Modern Bistro

Restrained hospitality. Deep basil + amber, SVG kitchen icons as category markers, Fraunces display with subtle steam animation on the logomark. Most editorial / grown-up of the three.
