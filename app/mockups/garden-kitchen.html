Summer Caprese Salad

prep 15 min cook none serves 4 tags italian · vegetarian · summer

Peak-summer tomatoes, torn mozzarella, and basil picked ten minutes ago. The dressing is just good olive oil and flaky salt — nothing between you and the ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb heirloom tomatoes, mixed sizes
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella, torn
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 3 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • Flaky sea salt
  • Black pepper, freshly cracked

Instructions

  1. Slice larger tomatoes into thick rounds; halve the small ones.
  2. Arrange tomato and mozzarella on a large platter, overlapping slightly.
  3. Tuck basil leaves throughout — don't chop, tear if large.
  4. Drizzle generously with olive oil.
  5. Finish with flaky salt and pepper. Serve immediately.
